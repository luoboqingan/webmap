Inroduction

This website serves as the job of the web mapping course and is used to introduce some basic conditions of the Isle of Wight. This website uses different map loading methods to display the basic information of the Isle of Wight. You can select different maps in the navigation bar to browse.

Home

What you see now is the homepage, where the basic information of the different maps and their specific functions are introduced.

Image mapping

A map of the Isle of Wight is placed in the image mapping. The map shows the main towns and transportation routes on the Isle of Wight.
The map can be zoomed in and out using the mouse wheel.

Google Map

The call of Google Maps is divided into two modules.
The first part is to show the geographical data of the center of the Isle of Wight. And some introduction information about the Isle of Wight.
The second part shows the main traffic routes on the Isle of Wight through KML files.

ESRI ArcGIS

This map shows more things. It includes settlements, iso-rainfall maps of the Isle of Wight, distribution of different types of soil and some major roads.

OpenLayers

In this map I loaded two layers, these two layers can choose whether to display. At the same time, a simple real-time display of the longitude and latitude of the mouse has been added.
